TechVision Club EVENTS
CodeUnscramble

This is a fun event where we will give you a piece of code in a programming language. The code that has been given to you will be jumbled up, all you need to do is make sense of this code by placing all the bits and pieces of the code, in the right way to get the desired output.

Web Scorer

Code Quibbler

Are you a perfectionist who has an obsession to do things the in the right way?. Wait no more, come and show us you ability to write the cleanest and fastest running code.

Andha Code

Did you ever try to close your eyes and walked blindly around in correct direction? Well you may have tried that but have you ever tried to code blind? We bet you that it will be more exciting than blind walk. So for the first time we are introducing Andha Code, where you have to solve the problem with the machine blinded. Come and experiment the blind way of coding and hit the blind spot to win big!.

Code Lego

Want to have fun while coding? Come and experience unseen side if coding where you can taste fun, going and lateral thinking all in one plate. So here comes Code Lego, a unique combination of crossword with code which engages you to solve coding question to fill in the crossword. A cross platform competition to test you puzzle solving and coding skills.

Paper Presentation

Have any creative, amazing idea? Want to present and publish them like a paper or journal but do not know how to approach? Do not worry! We are bringing you an opportunity to take the first step to achieve your goal. Team TechVision brings you paper presentation which helps you to present your topic in front of audience and experts and gain experience and advice to present at real conferences. Grab the opportunity and help yourself!

C pump

Dreaming about building your career as a programmer? Then get your basics right. Brush up your concepts of C by participating in the C Pump event which is bought to you by team rigolade for freshers to pump up your forte and stay ahead of your peers.

Quiz Buzz

Every fest has a quiz but the quiz that Techvision club is coming up with will drive you crazy. So we present you Quiz Buzz which has interesting quiz questions on many topics. Come and participate to win amazing prizes.
